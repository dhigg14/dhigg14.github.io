Website Evaluations

GameStop

GameStop Corp.is an American video game, consumer electronics, and gaming merchandise retailer. The company is headquartered in Grapevine, Texas, and is the largest video game retailer worldwide.

  • Contrast: There are two colors that the website uses which is red and white which matches because the logo uses these colors. The body of the page is also white and red, but the header stands out due to the bold font. The whole website is very clean and has a nice flow.
  • Repitition: The layout is very consistant repition, the fonts are very consistant throughout the website and it uses sans serif.
  • Alignment:The Alignments are very natural as each row have the games or consoles listed very orderly like nothing is out of line.
  • Proximity: The elements of this website is actually pretty neat as everything is in a rectangle or square. It allows people to organize from different products because usually consoles are labeled with rectangles and games are usually in squares.

Yahoo

Yahoo inc. is a global media and tech company connecting people to their passions and it can be for news and searching other stuff.

When I click on a link everything seems very spaced out and it just doesn't feel good. They also sometimes have different color for a random word and this happens alot.
